Who am I

Research Assistant and master's student at Oulu University, based in Oulu, Finland. Passionate problem solver with a Software Engineer background. My experience is mainly focusing on Full-Stack development around cloud-based ECM systems.

Java has been my main coding language since I started my journey in the Software Development field. Developing back-end solutions with Java, Spring Framework, Feign, RESTful Services and JUnit is my strongest skill set. Python, C++ or C belong to my stack as well.

I have developed a Smart Garden IoT system for plant monitoring with C++ and Arduino. Python I have used for Machine Learning related tasks in my current job and studies.

Highly eager to learn and improve myself, I decided to continue my studies at the University of Oulu. I started my Master of Computer Science and Engineering studies in autumn 2021, specialising in Applied Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

Alongside the studies, I keep gaining experience around Machine Learning, Cloud- and Edge-based systems while working as a research assistant for the EU Fractal project at the University of Oulu.
